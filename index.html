The Bored App

Welcome to the Bored App! The Bored App will randomly present you with an activity to do - usually a new activity you can learn that will make your day just a bit more exciting. Once you get your activity, if you would like to do it, then click Find Out How and the Bored App will return google results for how to do or learn that activity or skill so you can get started.
